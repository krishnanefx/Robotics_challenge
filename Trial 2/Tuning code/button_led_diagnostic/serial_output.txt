Button/LED diagnostic
Pins use INPUT_PULLUP, so pressed should read LOW.
Pin 49 = mode button, pin 22 = bumper 1, pin 33 = bumper 2.
LED output follows bumpers only: green while pin 22/33 is pressed, red otherwise.
BOOT | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released
POLL | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released
POLL | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released

--- pressed the mode button (pin 49) ---
CHANGE | pin49 mode=LOW/PRESSED pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released
POLL | pin49 mode=LOW/PRESSED pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released

--- released the mode button ---
CHANGE | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released
POLL | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released

--- pressed left bumper (pin 22): LED goes green ---
CHANGE | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=LOW/PRESSED pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released
POLL | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=LOW/PRESSED pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released

--- released left bumper: LED back to red ---
CHANGE | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released

--- pressed right bumper (pin 33): LED goes green ---
CHANGE | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=LOW/PRESSED
POLL | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=LOW/PRESSED

--- pressed both bumpers at once ---
CHANGE | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=LOW/PRESSED pin33 bumper2=LOW/PRESSED
POLL | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=LOW/PRESSED pin33 bumper2=LOW/PRESSED

--- released both: LED red, all inputs HIGH again ---
CHANGE | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=LOW/PRESSED
CHANGE | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released
POLL | pin49 mode=HIGH/released pin22 bumper1=HIGH/released pin33 bumper2=HIGH/released
